Services

Recruiting is Art and we are the Artists

Who?

Silicon Staffing is a leading career recruiting firm placing software and hardware developers in high-tech companies located in the Silicon Beach, Silicon Valley and throughout California. Our full-service team specializes in placing “impact players” in this technology driven era.

Why?

Silicon Staffing consists of a network of recruiting and candidate sourcing professionals. With an initial focus on senior-level recruiting followed up by our thorough pre-screening interview process. This process assures our business associates that we present only qualified candidates for their open requisitions. Our goal is to save employers valuable time, while assisting in the successful hiring of top candidates.
